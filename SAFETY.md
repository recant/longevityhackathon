# KinSpan — safety & responsible use

KinSpan is a **wellness and functional aging** tool for families. It is not a medical device.

- Do not use scores to diagnose, treat, or decide whether someone needs emergency care.
- Functional age estimates are **educational metaphors**, not laboratory biological age.
- Biomarkers can vary daily (sleep, stress, medications, flooring, shoes).
- Obtain consent before timing or filming a parent.
- Encourage professional care when the family is worried — frame the app as a conversation starter, not an authority.

UI copy avoids disease labels and uses trajectory language (improving / stable / watch closely).
